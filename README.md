## 🎯 Research Project: AI-Powered SEO Strategy
The primary goal is to shift from generic "AI-spam" to **"AI-Assisted Human Authority"**—producing content that AI search engines (Google AIO, Perplexity) prioritize for citations in the "Citation Economy."

### What I Collected
I have compiled a comprehensive research library consisting of:
*   **Video Insights:** Transcripts and detailed summaries from 10 SEO authorities, focusing on practical implementation and ranking proofs.
*   **LinkedIn Intelligence:** Scraped and collected expert POVs to track real-time shifts in GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) trends.
*   **Systematic Workflows:** Step-by-step guides on using AI automation (n8n, Claude Artifacts, Semrush).

### Why These Experts?
These 10 experts were selected because they represent the "front line" of SEO evolution. They were chosen based on:
1.  **Practical Ranking Evidence:** Strategies backed by verifiable working proofs and live ranking data.
2.  **Visibility over Rankings:** Targeting the "Citation Economy" where being the source of truth for AI is more valuable than a standard blue link.
3.  * **Editorial Integrity:** Prioritizing high-quality, trustworthy content that meets Google's standards (E-E-A-T). We focus on creating genuine and valuable content that naturally builds trust with both human readers and search engines, rather than trying to bypass AI detection.

### Research Validation
I have thoroughly reviewed and synthesized these sources to ensure deep alignment with AI-powered SEO objectives. Throughout this process, I prioritized experts who offer **verifiable working proofs**—ensuring every workflow in this system is grounded in practical application, not just speculative theory.

## Workflow Framework
1. **Identify Gaps:** Use AI-powered search analysis to find visibility opportunities.
2. **Structural Design:** Build schemas and hierarchies optimized for Generative Engines (GEO).
3. **Drafting & Refining:** Draft with AI, refine with "Human Layer" (experience, data, and unique POV).
4. **Distribution:** Deploy content across multiple platforms to build long-term topical authority.
